Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

PCA is used to reduce the amount of dimensions when displaying data. In our case, the data contains 4 attributes, which cannot be displayed visually on a 3 dimensional plane. Instead, PCA is used to summarize the attributes of our data and visualize them on a 2D and 3D plane. Below is an example of a 3D PCA for our multiclass dataset.
